pilau

Ingredients

    2 cups rice 6 medium potatoes (peeled and halved)
  • 2 onions sliced
  • 1 capsicum diced (optional)
  • 3 Cardamom Pods 1/2 teaspoon whole black pepper
  • 2 - 3 sticks whole cinnamon
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 teaspoon whole cumin
  • 1kg Mbuzi Mix (pre-boiled)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic ginger paste
  • 3 tablespoon HalisiFry

  • Instructions

    1. prepare ungredients together to avoid wasting time .
    2. fry onion till golden brown.
    3. Add garlic and ginger and fry.
    4. Add the whole pilau masala and cook till the aroma fills the house.
    5. Add the pre-boiled beef and fry till goiden brown.
    6. Add your potatoes and cook for ten minutes but do not cook fully.
