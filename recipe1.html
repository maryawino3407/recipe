Pilau

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 pound beef
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • Spices: cinnamon, cardamom, cloves

Instructions

  1. Cook beef until tender.
  2. Fry onions and spices until fragrant.
  3. Add rice and water, then cook until done.
